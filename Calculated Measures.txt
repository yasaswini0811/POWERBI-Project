Calculated Measures 

1. Average Rating = AVERAGE('IMDB-Movie-Data'[Rating])

2. Average Runtime = AVERAGE('IMDB-Movie-Data'[Runtime (Minutes)])

3. Average Votes = AVERAGE('IMDB-Movie-Data'[Votes])

4. Avg IMDb Rating = AVERAGE('IMDB-Movie-Data'[Rating])

5. Avg Meta score = AVERAGE('IMDB-Movie-Data'[Meta score])

6. Highest Movie Revenue = MAX ( 'IMDB-Movie-Data'[Revenue (Millions)] )

7. Movie Count = COUNT('IMDB-Movie-Data'[Title])

8. Movies by Year = COUNTROWS('IMDB-Movie-Data')

9. Revenue by Year = SUM('IMDB-Movie-Data'[Revenue (Millions)])

10. Total Movies = COUNTROWS('IMDB-Movie-Data')

11. Total Revenue = SUM('IMDB-Movie-Data'[Revenue (Millions)])

12. Total Votes = SUM('IMDB-Movie-Data'[Votes])

13. Unique Actors = DISTINCTCOUNT ( 'IMDB-Movie-Data'[Actors] )


Calculated Columns 

1. Actors

2. Description

3. Director

4. Genre

5. Meta score

6. Rank

7. Rating

8. Revenue (Millions)

9. Runtime (Minutes)

10. Title

11. Votes

12. Year
